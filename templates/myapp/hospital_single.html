About Square Hospital

Square Hospital is one of the leading private hospitals in Bangladesh. Established with the vision to provide world-class healthcare services, it offers a comprehensive range of medical services including cardiology, neurology, orthopedics, and pediatrics.

The hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art medical technology and staffed by a team of highly qualified doctors and nurses dedicated to patient care. It is renowned for its patient-centric approach and high standards of hygiene and safety.
