About Me

Dr. Fatima Rahman is a highly experienced Senior Cardiologist with over 15 years of practice in diagnosing and treating cardiovascular diseases. She specializes in interventional cardiology and heart failure management.

She completed her MBBS from Dhaka Medical College and later pursued her MD in Cardiology from the National Institute of Cardiovascular Diseases. She is known for her patient-centered approach and dedication to providing the highest standard of care.
